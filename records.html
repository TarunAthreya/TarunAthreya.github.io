Career Records

This page contains information about all of the records he has broken in his career, and what records still stand today.

Some of his many records include:

Oldest No. 1
Became the oldest-ever ATP world No. 1 at age 36.
Wimbledon
Most Wimbledon men's singles titles (8).
Consecutive Weeks at No. 1
Held the world No. 1 ranking for 237 consecutive weeks.
