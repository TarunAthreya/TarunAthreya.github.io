Career Overview

This page contains a timeline of his career and a broad overview of his accomplishments.

Major Milestones

2003
Won his first Grand Slam title at Wimbledon.
2004
Won three out of the four Grand Slam tournaments.
2009
Completed the Career Grand Slam by winning the French Open.
2018
Won his 20th and final Grand Slam title at the Australian Open.

His career spanned more than two decades and included 103 total titles.
