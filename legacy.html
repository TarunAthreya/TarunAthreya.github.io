Legacy and Impact

Roger Federer's impact on tennis is immeasurable. He changed the way the game was played with his fluid style and all-court game.

Rivalries

A huge part of his legacy is his iconic rivalries with other all-time greats.

His rivalry with Rafael Nadal is considered by many to be one of the greatest in sports history.

He also had a historic rivalry with Novak Djokovic, producing many classic matches.
